Tap to Paint

One tap fills a region with your chosen colour. Tap again to clear it. Zoom in to reach the smallest places.

Live Counter

A counter ring shows how much of the map you've covered, with little celebrations at 25%, 50%, 75% and 100%.

Multiple Lists

Keep separate maps for different trips, people, or goals — each with its own name and colour.

Share as a Poster

Export your map as a clean poster image, ready for stories and feeds. "Make your own map too."
